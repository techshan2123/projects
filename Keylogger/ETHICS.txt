# Ethics

## Purpose
This project exists to teach detection and defence. It is a simulator only.

## Allowable use
- Learn on your own device with your informed consent.
- Read the code and adapt defensive ideas.

## Not allowed
- No surveillance of other people.
- No operation outside the demo window or test textbox.
- No attempts to bypass security tools.
- No commercial use.

## Legal
Keystroke interception may be illegal. Check your local law before any experiment.

If you need help understanding safe practice send me a message.
